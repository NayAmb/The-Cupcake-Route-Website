About The Cupcake Route

The Cupcake Route is a mobile cupcake truck that brings freshly baked desserts to communities across California.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create delicious cupcakes that bring people together no matter the occasion.

What Makes Us Special

Every cupcake is made from scratch with high quality ingredients. We offer rotating flavors and creative designs that make each visit exciting.
